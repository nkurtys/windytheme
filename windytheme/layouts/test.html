Making Microbiology Data FAIR and Open

As a part of the Nationale Forschungsdaten Infrastruktur (NFDI), NFDI4Microbiota wants to support the microbiology community with access to data, analysis services, data/metadata standards, and training.

Learn more


What we offer

Our consortium offers a wide range of services, knowledge, and best practices. Below you find the main topics on which we support our community.

Training
An important goal is the support of the microbiology community by education and training

Services
To support your research we offer services such as StrainInfo and VirJenDB

Helpdesk
Questions about data management, data analysis and NFDI4Microbiota will be answered here

Community Engagement

Funding
